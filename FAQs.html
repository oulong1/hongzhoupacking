Do solar panels require maintenance?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Occasional cleaning to remove dirt and debris is usually enough to keep them running efficiently. We also offer maintenance packages for long-term care.

How much can I save with solar energy?

The amount you save depends on your location, energy usage, and the size of your solar system. On average, most homeowners see significant savings on their electricity bills over time.

What is the lifespan of solar panels?

Solar panels typically have a lifespan of 25-30 years. Even after this period, they often continue to produce energy, albeit at a slightly reduced efficiency.
