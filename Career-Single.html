Solar Technician

Job Overview:

We are seeking a skilled and dedicated Solar Technician to join our team. As a Solar Technician, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and troubleshooting solar panel systems. Your role will involve working closely with customers and ensuring that all installations meet safety and performance standards. If you have hands-on experience with solar technology and a passion for renewable energy, we encourage you to apply.

Job Responsibilities:

As a Solar Technician, your responsibilities will include:

  • Installing, maintaining, and repairing solar panel systems.
  • Conducting site assessments to determine the optimal setup for solar systems.
  • Ensuring that installations comply with safety and quality standards.
  • Troubleshooting system performance issues and performing routine maintenance.
  • Providing excellent customer service and educating customers on system operations.

Qualifications & Skills:

To succeed in this role, you'll need:

  • Experience in installing and maintaining solar panel systems.
  • Understanding of electrical wiring and safety protocols.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work at heights and in various weather conditions.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to explain technical concepts to customers.
